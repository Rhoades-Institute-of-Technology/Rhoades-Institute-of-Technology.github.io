Projects

Technology tools, model legislation, hardware prototypes, and AI systems — built in the open to make civic participation practical and accessible.

Featured Project

TVN Bridge

Alpha

A local-first AI governance platform that enforces constraints AI cannot bypass. Sovereign Firewall, Context Gate, Truth Nodes, and universal audit trails — with zero telemetry. The backbone of RIT's technology ecosystem.

TVN Vault

Alpha

An encrypted local workspace — notes, databases, and double-entry accounting with SQLCipher encryption. No cloud, no telemetry. Your passphrase is the only key.

No Solos Alpha

Alpha

A community raid boss game for conventions and events. Attendees scan a QR code, pick a hero class, and join the fight against OUTRAGE — an evil corpo AI boss on the big screen. Vanilla JavaScript, hand-drawn sprites, runs on a phone. The spectacle is the conversation starter; the mission is what comes after.

PTAA

Draft Framework

The Political Transparency and Accountability Act — a model legislative framework requiring real-time PAC donor disclosure, banning anonymous pass-through contributions, and establishing a Citizen Oversight Corps for public auditing of political finance.

policytransparencylegislation

SORS

Active

The Snow Operations Reporting System makes invisible labor visible through structured data collection. It documents snow removal work — conditions, time spent, equipment used — turning an undervalued job into a measurable, accountable record.

datalaborreporting

Sentry / Snow Stakes

Prototype

A hardware companion to SORS — physical markers and sensor systems that support snow operations reporting with real-world reference points for documentation and accountability.

hardwareIoTcompanion

Wayfinder

In Development

AI-guided learning layers for open-source creative tools like Godot, GIMP, and Blender. Inspired by gaming overlays like Deadly Boss Mods — Wayfinder highlights the next action in real-time, gives context on why, and waits for the human to execute. Guided mastery, not automation.

Nimbus

In Development

A civic literacy AI coach that translates complex policy, legislation, and government processes into accessible language. Built to be a research companion, not a replacement for critical thinking.
