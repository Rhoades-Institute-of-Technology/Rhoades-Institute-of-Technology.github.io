No Solos Alpha

A cooperative multiplayer game built around one rule: no solo play. Every mechanic requires teamwork, communication, and shared strategy.

Alpha — In Development

What It Is

No Solos is a browser-based cooperative raid game where players take on roles — tank, healer, damage — and fight boss encounters together in real time. There is no single-player mode. Every ability, every mechanic, every victory requires coordination with other people.

The game is designed as both entertainment and a proof of concept: that collaborative problem-solving can be fun, accessible, and built entirely in the open. The boss AI uses phased attack patterns that force role-specific responses — you can't brute force your way through alone.

How It Works

Browser-Based

No downloads. Open a link and play. Works on desktop and mobile.

Real-Time Co-op

WebSocket multiplayer. See your team, coordinate attacks, heal each other live.

Role-Based

Tank, healer, DPS — classic MMO roles adapted for quick pickup sessions.

Current Status

No Solos Alpha is in active development. The game engine is built — 15 modules, boss AI with phased attack patterns, hero spawning and combat, support resurrection mechanics, and a 17-layer render system.

The server currently runs on-demand for playtesting and events. A persistent online version is planned for launch at MGC 2026 (April 24-26) or shortly after.

If you want to playtest when the server is live, join the gaming Discord — that's where sessions get announced.
